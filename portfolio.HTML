Soft Robotics: Soft robotics focuses on creating robots with flexible and compliant structures that mimic natural organisms. These robots are more adaptable to complex environments and safer to interact with humans.This involves coordinating large numbers of robots to work together towards a common goal. Swarm robotics allows for tasks that are too complex or hazardous for a single robot to accomplish alone, such as search and rescue missions or environmental monitoring.AI enables robots to learn from and adapt to their environment. Machine learning algorithms allow robots to improve their performance over time, making them more efficient and capable of complex tasks.Exoskeletons are wearable robotic devices that augment human strength and endurance.
